ODISHA

Odisha, located on the eastern coast of India, is known for its rich cultural heritage and ancient temples, including the famous Jagannath Temple in Puri. The state boasts a diverse landscape, with a long coastline along the Bay of Bengal, lush green forests, and picturesque hills in regions like the Eastern Ghats.
