PUNJAB

Punjab, a northern Indian state, is renowned for its rich cultural heritage and vibrant traditions. With a history dating back centuries, it has been a melting pot of cultures and civilizations. Known as the "Land of Five Rivers" due to the Beas, Chenab, Jhelum, Ravi, and Sutlej rivers that flow through it, Punjab boasts fertile plains that have made it the "Granary of India
